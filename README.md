#  Global Electric Vehicle (EV) Sales Analysis | Power BI Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **global Electric Vehicle (EV) sales data** from 2005 to 2023 using **Microsoft Power BI**.
The goal is to explore EV adoption trends, regional performance, and key insights on modes, powertrains, and market share to support **data-driven sustainability decisions**.

---

##  Objectives

* Analyze global EV sales growth from 2005 to 2023.
* Identify top-performing regions and countries in EV adoption.
* Compare EV market share by **mode (Car, Bus, 2/3-wheeler, Truck)** and **powertrain type (BEV, PHEV, FCEV)**.
* Generate actionable insights through interactive data visualization.

##  Key Insights

* **EV adoption has surged globally since 2018**, with notable growth in China and Europe.
* **BEVs dominate the market**, showing consistent year-over-year growth.
* **Cars account for the largest share**, followed by **buses and 2/3-wheelers**.
* **Asia leads in EV sales**, while Europe shows the highest EV market share percentage.
* **Sharp increase in EV stock** indicates strong consumer shift towards clean mobility.
